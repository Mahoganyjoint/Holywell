Events

Every now and then we try to organise a different experience at The Holywell inn, ranging from themed dining evenings to quiz nights, live music events or even barbeques.​ We dress the pub to suit the occasion and sometimes encourage our customers to dress according to the theme of the evening - it all adds to the atmosphere and enjoyment.

St George's Day

This year we are celebrating St George's Day by dressing the pub with patriotic decorations and we will be serving a wonderful selection of traditional English meals on our specials menu for the evening, details to be available shortly.

Pie and Pud Night

We are having another of our famous Pie and Pud nights. On this evening we will be serving a wide choice of pies including vegetarian and gluten free choices, all served with a choice of potatoes and vegetables and followed by a delicious range of home made puddings.

Morris Dancing

Mayhem Border Morris dancers are returning to The Holywell Inn this evening to entertain all who wish to attend. After the dancing finishes you are more than welcome to join them inside whilst they consume a well earned drink or two and continue the fun with folk music and singing.
